🎮 Games — two per session

Digital game (session1_game.html … session6_game.html): Mixed click, tap, and drag activities covering vocabulary, reading comprehension, and grammar. 3 rounds per session with score and star rating. Works on tablet and computer.

Printable game (game1.html … game6.html): Physical card, board, or bingo games to cut out and play. No screen needed. Designed for 2–4 players. Research-backed formats: Guess Who (S1), Roll-and-Speak board (S2 & S6), Picture Bingo (S3 & S4), Memory card pairs (S5).
