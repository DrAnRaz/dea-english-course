Animal Memory Match! 🦁🃏
Match the animal to its superpower!

📋 How to Play (Memory / Concentration)

  1. Print ALL pages of cards (pages 2, 3, and optionally page 4). Cut out all 20 cards carefully along the dashed lines.
  2. Mix all cards together and place them FACE DOWN in a 4×5 grid on a table.
  3. Players take turns flipping 2 cards face up for everyone to see.
  4. If the animal card matches its ability card → it's a PAIR! Keep them and go again.
  5. If they don't match → turn them back face down. Try to remember where they are!
  6. BONUS RULE: When you find a pair, say the FULL sentence on the ability card out loud! 🗣️
  7. The player with the MOST pairs at the end WINS! 🏆

⚡ Challenge Version — Play Alone!

Lay all 20 cards face down. Try to match all 10 pairs in under 10 turns! Each turn = flipping 2 cards. Can you beat your own record?

💡 Memory TipMake a mental picture of each animal when you flip its card. Connect the image to the location on the grid!
📖 Language TipEach ability card uses "can" and "can't." Read them aloud — this is English practice too!
🎨 Colour ClueEach pair shares the same border colour. If two cards look the same colour — check if they're a match!
