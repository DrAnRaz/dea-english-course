👋 Hey Dea! Today YOU are the main character.

You're going to make your very own English ID card — like a superhero profile, but for YOU! 🦸‍♀️

Look at the 12 words below. They are going to describe YOU and your family. Before we start, circle 2 words that are most like you:

😄 funny 💛 kind 🙈 shy 🧠 clever ⚽ sporty 🎨 creative 📏 tall 🤝 friendly
💡 Did you know? In English, you always need TWO words: "I am" — you can NEVER just say "am funny" without the "I"! It's like a rule: subject + verb, always together. 🔗

Your goal today: By the end of this session, you will introduce yourself in English. Ready? Let's go! 🚀

Dea writes: Two words that describe me are…
⚡ Warm-Up — True or False? 5–10 min

How to play

  1. Say statements about yourself. Dea reacts: 👍 = True, 👎 = False.
  2. Examples: "I am a teacher." / "I am twelve years old." / "I am very funny."
  3. Switch — Dea makes statements, you react.
  4. After 5–6 rounds, introduce today's adjectives by labeling what you acted out.

📖 Reading — Meet Emma and Her Family 10–15 min

Hi! My name is Emma. I am twelve years old. I am from London, in England. I am tall and I have long brown hair and green eyes. My friends say I am funny and creative. I love art, music, and dancing. I am very friendly — I talk to everyone at school!

My mum's name is Claire. She is a teacher at my school. She is kind and very patient with her students. She is not very tall, but she is beautiful and elegant. My dad's name is Tom. He is a doctor at the local hospital. He is tall and sometimes serious at work, but at home he is funny and playful. He tells the best jokes!

I have a sister named Lucy. She is eight years old. She is small and quiet. At school, she is shy and not very confident. But on the football pitch, she is completely different — she is fast, brave, and amazing!

I also have a brother. His name is Jack. He is sixteen. He is sporty and very smart. He studies a lot and he wants to be an engineer. He is sometimes a little arrogant, but he is also helpful and funny when he wants to be.

My best friend is Maya. She is from Jamaica. She is creative, energetic, and very confident. We are both in the art club. Maya is definitely not shy — she is the funniest and loudest person in our school!

1. How old is Emma?
2. What is Emma's mum's job?
3. Who is the sporty person in the family?
4. Find TWO adjectives that describe Jack.
5. 🔍 Inference: Why do you think Lucy is "brave" at football but "not very confident" at school?
6. 📖 Vocabulary: Find a word in the text that means "full of energy".
7. 💬 Your turn: Which person in the text is most similar to you? Why?

✏️ Follow-up — Color-code the text

  • 🔵 Blue pen: Underline all am / is / are
  • 🔴 Red pen: Circle all adjectives
  • Count together: How many adjectives? How many is/am?
