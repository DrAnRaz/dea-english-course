⚡ Warm-Up — Point and Name 5–10 min

How to play

  1. Draw a rough town map — a few squares, a road, a park. Point to each building and say: "This is a park. What's this?"
  2. Dea tries to name each one in English. You give the word if she doesn't know it.
  3. Write each word on the map as you name it.
Recycling S3: "Is there a park near your house?" — recycles there is and previews today's prepositions.

📖 Reading — "Welcome to Millbrook!" 10–15 min

Welcome to Millbrook!

Welcome to Millbrook, a beautiful small town in England! Let me tell you about all the great places here.

In the centre of Millbrook, there is a big park. The park has a fountain in the middle and lots of green trees. Next to the park, there is a lovely café. The café has tables outside in summer, and people love to sit there and drink coffee.

Opposite the park, there is the town hall. It is a big white building with tall columns. Between the town hall and the café, there is a car park — very useful for visitors!

Near the car park, there is a supermarket. The supermarket is open every day from eight o'clock in the morning. In front of the supermarket, there is a bus stop. People wait there for the bus to the city.

The school is behind the park. It is a red brick building with a big playground. There are two football pitches and a basketball court. Next to the school, there is a small library. The library has more than ten thousand books!

In Millbrook, there is also a cinema, a hospital, a post office, and three restaurants. The cinema is between the library and the hospital. There is no shopping mall in Millbrook — it is a small, quiet town. But there are lots of friendly people and it is a wonderful place to live!

Would you like to live in Millbrook? What is there in your town?

🔧 Grammar — there is/are + Prepositions 10–15 min

"Remember from Session 3? There IS for one thing. There ARE for more than one. Now we combine it with prepositions to make real descriptions!"

Visual: the full pattern

✅ Positive❌ Negative❓ Question
ONE thingThere is a park.There isn't a cinema.Is there a hospital?
MANY thingsThere are two cafés.There aren't any buses.Are there any shops?

Now add prepositions: There is a library next to the park.

There are two cafés opposite the cinema.

Is there a bus stop near the school? — Yes, there is!

⚡ Challenge Round 10–12 min

Fill the Preposition 🔍

Hint: next to | between | opposite | in front of | behind | near

1. "The cinema is ___ the library ___ the hospital."
2. "The bus stop is ___ the supermarket."
3. "The school is ___ the park."
4. "The café is ___ the park."

Answers: 1. between / and  |  2. in front of  |  3. behind  |  4. next to

Negative Forms 🔄

Hint: there is → there isn't  |  there are → there aren't any

"There is a cinema." → "There ___ a cinema in my town."
"There are restaurants." → "There ___ any restaurants."
"There is a shopping mall." → "There ___ a shopping mall in Millbrook." (true! It's small)

Write Your Own ✍️

Write a paragraph (4–5 sentences) about your town or a town you know. Use 4 different prepositions and there is / there are.

Example: In my town, there is a big school. Next to the school, there is a park…
