My Town — Roll and Describe!

A board game about prepositions and town places

🎮 How to Play

1
2 to 4 players. You need: 1 dice and 1 small token per player.
2
Roll the dice and move your token forward along the streets.
3
When you land on a BUILDING square, say where it is using a preposition:
"The school is BEHIND the park."
4
If your sentence is correct → STAY on the square ✓
5
If your sentence is wrong → go BACK 1 square ✗
6
CHALLENGE SQUARES: Follow the special instructions on yellow/red squares.
7
First player to reach FINISH wins! 🏆

🏆 Square Types

Blue squares — Normal building. Describe its location!
Gold squares — Lucky! Roll again or jump forward.
Red squares — Challenge! Miss a turn or go back.
Green square — START HERE!
Gold gradient — FINISH! Say 3 sentences to win!

⚡ Challenge Mode

Describe TWO buildings in one sentence for an extra turn!

Example: "The library is NEXT TO the school, and the cinema is BETWEEN the library and the hospital."
