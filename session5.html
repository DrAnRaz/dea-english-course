📖 Reading — "Incredible Animals" 10–15 min
Incredible Animals

Our planet is full of amazing animals. Let me tell you about some of my favourites!

Dolphins are incredible. They live in the ocean, but they are not fish — they are mammals! Dolphins can swim very fast, up to 60 kilometres per hour. They can jump high out of the water. They can also communicate with each other using special sounds called clicks and whistles. Dolphins can't breathe underwater — they come to the surface to breathe through a hole on top of their heads.

Eagles are powerful birds. They can fly very high — sometimes higher than mountains! Eagles have amazing eyes. They can see a small mouse from 500 metres away. They can fly at 130 kilometres per hour when they dive for food. Eagles can't run fast on the ground, but they don't need to!

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals in the world. They can run at 120 kilometres per hour. That is faster than a car on a normal road! Cheetahs can't roar like lions, but they can purr like cats. They can also climb trees, but they prefer to run.

Penguins are birds, but they can't fly. They can swim brilliantly, though — they use their wings as flippers in the water. Penguins can hold their breath for up to 20 minutes. They live in cold places and they can survive temperatures of minus 60 degrees Celsius!

Every animal has special abilities. What is your favourite animal and what can it do?
1. Are dolphins fish or mammals?
2. How fast can cheetahs run?
3. What do dolphins use to communicate?
4. What is special about eagle eyes?
🔍 5. Inference: Why don't eagles need to run fast on the ground?
📖 6. Vocabulary: Dolphins breathe through a special hole. What is this called? (blowhole — look it up or ask your teacher!)
💬 7. Your turn: Which animal in the text do you find most amazing? Why?

🔧 Grammar — can / can't for Ability 10–15 min

"'Can' is a magic helper word. It never changes — no -s, no -ed. Just 'can' for everyone, every time."

Visual: can ✅ vs can't ❌

✅ CAN
Dolphins can swim fast.
Eagles can fly high.
Parrots can speak!
She can swim. (no -s!)
❌ CAN'T
Fish can't walk.
Butterflies can't swim.
I can't fly! 😄
He can't climb. (no -s!)

Hungarian note: "Tud repülni" = "can fly" — very similar concept! The difference: in English questions, the word order changes: "Can it fly?" not "It can fly?" Remind Dea of this one difference only.
