Guess Who? 🌟

Me and My World — A Speaking Game Using Adjectives!
📋 How to Play

How to Play (2 Players)

  1. 1Print and cut out all 12 character cards below. Keep the Question Starter Card too!
  2. 2Each player secretly picks ONE character card. Don't show your partner!
  3. 3Take turns asking YES / NO questions about adjectives.
    Examples: "Is your person tall?"  ·  "Is she funny?"  ·  "Is he brave?"
  4. 4Your partner answers: "Yes, she is!" or "No, he isn't."
  5. 5First player to correctly GUESS the other's character WINS! 🏆

💡 Tips for Good Questions

  • Ask about one adjective at a time
  • Remember answers to narrow it down
  • Try: "Is she...?" / "Is he...?" / "Are they...?"
  • Use the Question Starter Card if you need ideas!
