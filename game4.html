Food Bingo! 🎯🍕
A vocabulary and speaking game about food

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Print ONE bingo card for each player (Cards A, B, C, or D).
  2. The teacher (or one player) reads sentences from the Caller List.
  3. Listen carefully! Find the FOOD mentioned in the sentence on your card. Mark it!
  4. First player with 4 in a row — across, down, or diagonal — shouts "BINGO!"
  5. IMPORTANT RULE: To WIN, you must say your OWN sentence about one of the foods in your row!
    Good example: "I love pizza!" or "I don't like broccoli."
    Not accepted: just saying the food name.
  6. Play again and try to get a full house (all 16 squares)!

🗣️ Speaking Challenge

Can you say a sentence for EVERY food on your card?
Use: I love…, I really like…, I don't like…, I absolutely hate…
