My Day — Roll and Speak! 🎲⏰

A board game about daily routines

📋 How to Play

  1. 2 to 4 players
  2. You need: 1 dice and 1 coin (or small token) per player
  3. Roll the dice and move your token forward
  4. When you land on a square, say a FULL SENTENCE about the picture!
    ⏰ Time square (orange): "Sara wakes up at 7 o'clock."
    🖼️ Routine square (yellow): Say what the person is doing
  5. If your sentence is correct: STAY on the square ✅
  6. If your sentence is wrong: go BACK 1 square ⬅️
  7. First player to reach FINISH wins! 🏆
