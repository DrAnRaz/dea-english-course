📖 Reading — "Our Perfect Classroom" 10–15 min
Our Perfect Classroom

Hi! I'm Emma. Today I want to tell you about my classroom. It is my favourite place in school!

Our classroom is big and bright. There is a large whiteboard at the front. The teacher writes vocabulary words on it every day. There are two windows on the left side — they are always open in summer because our classroom gets very warm.

There are twenty-five desks in our classroom. Each student has a desk and a chair. I sit next to Maya, my best friend. We have pencil cases on our desks. Mine has two pens, three pencils, a ruler, and an eraser. Maya has a big green pencil case with stickers on it!

There is a bookshelf at the back of the room. It has lots of books — dictionaries, reading books, and art books. There are also some science models on the shelf. My favourite is a model of the human eye.

Our teacher has a big desk at the front. She has a computer and lots of papers on it. There is also a plant next to her desk. The plant has beautiful red flowers.

On the walls, there are posters and maps. There is a world map, a grammar poster, and our class's artwork. Every student has a picture on the wall!

I love our classroom because it is colourful and friendly. Do you like your classroom?
1. Where is the whiteboard?
2. How many desks are in the classroom?
3. What does Emma have in her pencil case?
4. What is Emma's favourite science model?
5. 🔍 Inference: Why are the windows always open in summer?
6. 📖 Vocabulary: Find a word in the text that means "a large furniture piece that holds books".
7. 💬 Your turn: Describe something in YOUR classroom using "there is" or "there are".

Reading notes
🔧 Grammar — have/has + there is/there are 10–15 min

Visual: there IS (one) vs there ARE (many)

there IS
📋
= ONE thing
There is a whiteboard.
there ARE
🪑 🪑 🪑 🪑
= MORE THAN ONE
There are four desks.

have/has: I have · you have · she has · he has

there is/are: There is a whiteboard. · There are 20 desks.

Hungarian note: "Van egy tábla az osztályteremben" = There is a whiteboard in the classroom. Very similar structure! Hungarian speakers often find there is/are easier. Celebrate this win!

💡 Hint: Is it one thing or many things? Is it he/she/it?

Negative Forms 🔄

Change these positive sentences to negative. Fill in the blank.

1. There is a TV in the classroom. → There ___ a TV.
2. We have a swimming pool. → We ___ have a swimming pool.
3. The classroom has a piano. → The classroom ___ have a piano.
💡 Hint box: there is → there isn't  |  have → don't have  |  has → doesn't have

Write Your Own ✍️

Describe your classroom! Write 5 sentences using: there is, there are, have, has.
Example: There is a big window. I have a red pencil case.
